what is UX?

If you went to architecture school, you are already familiar with user experience (UX). From the process of analyzing a site, to details of how a door handle is at the optimal height, all these things culminated with an actual person experiencing our designs. Through our process of complex problem solving, we strived to create an experience that was functional, intuitive, and even delightful. Now apply that to digital products, such as websites and apps... the principles are the same, yet the technologies are different.

about me

Hi, I'm Jeremy. I'm a UX/UI Designer with a passion for creating well thought out digital solutions through exploring the intersection of culture, technology and the design process. I went to grad school for architecture at Rhode Island School of Design, where I survived on minimal sleep and vending machine food. After graduating, I knew the architecture lifestyle just wasn't for me, so I spent some time trying to find an alternative career where I could still use my newly developed skills.
